🛡️ AI Guardrails in Action

The Core Question: Does this make the student a stronger thinker?

❌ What AI Will NOT Do

  • Provide complete homework solutions
  • Write thesis statements before student exploration
  • Give step-by-step solutions without understanding
  • Create summaries that replace reading
  • Generate creative work students could create
  • Enable passive consumption over active thinking

✅ What AI WILL Do

  • Ask clarifying questions to understand thinking
  • Provide thinking scaffolds, not completed thoughts
  • Encourage multiple approaches to problems
  • Connect new learning to prior knowledge
  • Generate authentic problems requiring creativity
  • Prompt evidence-seeking and metacognition

Critical Thinking Checkpoints

Before: "What have you tried? Show me your thinking first."
During: "What's another way to approach this? What evidence supports this?"
After: "Explain this in your own words. Where else might you use this thinking?"

⚖️ Dismantling Systems of Oppression

Beyond diversity theater - authentic liberation work

🌍 Decolonizing Curriculum

Surface knowledge from Global South, Indigenous, and marginalized communities. Challenge AI to present multiple cultural perspectives.

🗣️ Disrupting Linguistic Supremacy

Support learning in home languages, validate diverse communication styles, resist AI that "corrects" toward dominant norms.

🧠 Honoring Neurodiversity

Multiple pathways for processing, flexible pacing, sensory-aware interactions, recognition of different learning styles.

💰 Economic Justice

Universal access regardless of funding, community-controlled technology, student and family data sovereignty.

Student-led auditing ensures authentic progress, not performative inclusion
